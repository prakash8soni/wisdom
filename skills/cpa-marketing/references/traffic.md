# CPA Traffic Sources Guide

## Social Media Traffic

### Instagram
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Cost:** Free (organic) or Paid (ads)

**Strategy:**
- Niche-specific accounts
- Story swipe-ups (10k+ followers)
- Reels for reach
- Bio link with landing page
- Engagement pods for boost

**Best Niches:** Fashion, fitness, gaming, lifestyle, beauty

**Posting Frequency:** 1-3 posts/day, 5-10 stories/day

**Tools:** Buffer, Later, Metricool

---

### TikTok
**Difficulty:** Easy
**Cost:** Free (organic) or Paid (ads)

**Strategy:**
- Trending sounds = more reach
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- CTA in caption and pinned comment
- Link in bio
- Post 3-5x daily

**Best Niches:** Gaming, entertainment, finance, education, lifestyle

**Posting Frequency:** 3-5 videos/day

**Tools:** CapCut, TokBackup, TikTok Creative Center

---

### YouTube
**Difficulty:** Medium-Hard
**Cost:** Free (organic) or Paid (ads)

**Strategy:**
- Long-form tutorials with locked resources
- Shorts for quick traffic
- Description links
- Pinned comment CTA
- End screen CTAs

**Best Niches:** Gaming, tutorials, reviews, how-to, entertainment

**Posting Frequency:** 1-2 long videos/week + daily shorts

**Tools:** TubeBuddy, VidIQ, YouTube Studio

---

### Twitter/X
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Cost:** Free (organic) or Paid (ads)

**Strategy:**
- Thread marketing
- Trending hashtag hijacking
- Reply to popular accounts
- Link in bio
- Twitter Blue for longer posts

**Best Niches:** Finance, crypto, tech, news, gaming

**Posting Frequency:** 5-10 tweets/day, 1 thread/week

**Tools:** Hypefury, TweetDeck, Typefully

---

### Pinterest
**Difficulty:** Medium
**Cost:** Free (organic) or Paid (ads)

**Strategy:**
- Visual pins with CTAs
- Idea pins (no links, brand awareness)
- Board optimization
- Rich pins with details
- Pinterest SEO

**Best Niches:** DIY, recipes, fashion, home decor, beauty

**Posting Frequency:** 10-20 pins/day

**Tools:** Tailwind, Canva, Pinterest Scheduler

---

### Facebook
**Difficulty:** Medium-Hard
**Cost:** Free (groups) or Paid (ads)

**Strategy:**
- Group marketing (provide value first)
- Page content
- Marketplace listings
- Facebook Reels
- Messenger bots

**Best Niches:** Local business, jobs, marketplace, groups, entertainment

**Posting Frequency:** 1-3 posts/day on page, 1-2 group posts/day

**Tools:** Meta Business Suite, ManyChat, Buffer

---

## Paid Traffic Sources

### Native Ads
**Networks:** Taboola, Outbrain, MGID, RevContent

**Cost:** $0.01 - $0.50 per click

**Strategy:**
- Clickbait headlines
- Native-looking content
- A/B test heavily
- Mobile-first landing pages

**Best For:** Sweepstakes, health, finance, viral content

---

### Push Notifications
**Networks:** PropellerAds, EvaDav, Adsterra, Pushground

**Cost:** $0.001 - $0.05 per click

**Strategy:**
- Catchy push titles
- Frequency capping
- Geo-targeting
- Device targeting

**Best For:** App installs, sweepstakes, downloads, dating

---

### Pop/Redirect Traffic
**Networks:** PropellerAds, PopAds, ZeroPark, Adsterra

**Cost:** $0.0005 - $0.01 per view

**Strategy:**
- High volume needed
- Mobile-focused
- Fast landing pages
- Aggressive offers

**Best For:** App installs, downloads, mobile content

---

### Search PPC
**Networks:** Google Ads, Bing Ads

**Cost:** $0.10 - $5.00+ per click

**Strategy:**
- Long-tail keywords
- Negative keyword lists
- Landing page quality score
- Ad copy optimization

**Best For:** High-payout offers (finance, insurance, B2B)

---

## Traffic Scaling Strategy

### Phase 1: Test (Days 1-7)
- Budget: $5-10/day
- Test 3-5 offers
- Test 2-3 traffic sources
- Goal: Find winning combination

### Phase 2: Optimize (Days 8-14)
- Budget: $10-25/day
- Focus on winners
- A/B test landing pages
- Goal: Improve ROI

### Phase 3: Scale (Week 3+)
- Budget: $50-500+/day
- Scale winning campaigns
- Add new traffic sources
- Automate everything possible
