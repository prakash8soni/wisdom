# CPA Offer Selection Guide

## Offer Types

### 1. Email Submit (Easiest)
- **Payout**: $0.50 - $2.00
- **Conversion**: High (15-30%)
- **Best For**: Beginners, social traffic
- **Example**: Win iPhone, Gift Card offers

### 2. Zip Submit
- **Payout**: $0.80 - $1.50
- **Conversion**: Very High (20-40%)
- **Best For**: US traffic, mobile
- **Example**: Insurance quotes, local deals

### 3. App Installs
- **Payout**: $0.50 - $5.00
- **Conversion**: Medium (10-20%)
- **Best For**: Mobile traffic, gaming
- **Example**: Mobile games, utility apps

### 4. Free Trial
- **Payout**: $20 - $50
- **Conversion**: Low (3-8%)
- **Best For**: Email lists, content sites
- **Example**: Weight loss, dating, software

### 5. Sale/Purchase
- **Payout**: $10 - $200+
- **Conversion**: Low (1-5%)
- **Best For**: Targeted traffic, email
- **Example**: E-commerce, subscriptions

---

## Selection Criteria

### Must Check
1. **EPC** - Earnings Per Click (higher = better)
2. **Conversion Rate** - % of clicks that convert
3. **Payout** - What you earn per conversion
4. **Network Reputation** - Do they pay on time?
5. **Offer Restrictions** - Geo, device, traffic source

### Red Flags
- ❌ Extremely high payout for simple action
- ❌ No EPC data available
- ❌ New network with no reviews
- ❌ Strict traffic restrictions you can't meet
- ❌ Weekly payout requirements (may shave leads)

## Offer Testing Strategy

### 100-Click Rule
Test each offer with at least 100 clicks before judging:
- **0 conversions** → Drop offer
- **1-2 conversions** → Test more (bad landing page?)
- **3+ conversions** → Scale up

### Rotation Testing
Run 3-5 offers simultaneously:
1. Equal traffic split
2. Track individual performance
3. Keep top 2 performers
4. Replace bottom 3 with new offers
5. Repeat

## Network Approval Tips

### Getting Approved
1. **Be honest** about traffic sources
2. **Have a plan** ready (niche, traffic, offers)
3. **Show experience** (even if new, show research)
4. **Professional approach** (proper English, clear answers)
5. **Call them** if possible (shows seriousness)

### Common Application Questions
- "Where will you promote our offers?"
- "How much experience do you have?"
- "What's your monthly budget?"
- "What traffic sources do you use?"
- "Do you have a website?"

### Good Answers
- "I run [niche] campaigns on [platform] with [method]"
- "I've been testing offers for [X months] and ready to scale"
- "I'm starting with $500-1000/month for testing"
- "Primarily [social platform] and [PPC/native/email]"
- "Yes, here's my site: [URL] or I use landing pages"
